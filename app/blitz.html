About

Post signing the contract, we become the exclusive rights holder for their professional engagements (activities/performances) across the world.

ITW's new, independent vertical is poised to disrupt traditional talent management offering agile, flexible, and end to end global talent solutions. We ensure high benefit-cost ratio for the event by screening the best talent and housing them appropriately during the event's execution.
